> Our vision is a world where AI systems are as reliable as the infrastructure they run on. We foresee a future where intelligent agents operate with unwavering consistency, seamlessly handling failures and scaling effortlessly to meet mission-critical demands without constant human intervention.

To realize this, we are pioneering the **reliability runtime for AI agents**: an open-source infrastructure layer that ensures your workflows are durable, resilient, and effortless to scale. Exosphere empowers you to turn experimental AI into production-grade systems that just work, today, tomorrow, and beyond.
